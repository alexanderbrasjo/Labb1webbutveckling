About us

Welcome to Brasjos Pizzeria, a cherished culinary landmark nestled in the heart of Kode, Sweden.
Our story is steeped in tradition and flavored with passion, as we have been crafting authentic Neapolitan pizzas for three generations.
From the humble beginnings of our great-grandfather, who ignited the first wood-fired oven in the 1950s, Brasjos Pizzeria has evolved into a beloved community hub.
We pride ourselves on continuing the family legacy, blending time-honored techniques with the freshest local ingredients to serve you pizzas that are not just meals, but a celebration of our rich heritage.
Our signature Neapolitan pizzas are a testament to our dedication to authenticity. Each pizza is a masterpiece, handcrafted with the finest flour, ripest tomatoes, and the creamiest mozzarella.
Cooked to perfection in our traditional wood-fired oven, these pizzas offer a slice of Naples right here in Kode.
As you step into our pizzeria, you're not just a customer; you're part of our extended family. We believe in creating an experience that transcends the ordinary.
It's not just about the food; it's about the warmth, the laughter, and the memories that are created around our tables.

We invite you to join us at Brasjos Pizzeria, where every pizza tells a story of family, tradition, and the timeless art of Italian cooking.
Come and share a meal with us, and become a part of a legacy that has been lovingly nurtured through generations.
